🛑 ReelGuard — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

ReelGuard ("the App") is a free Android application that helps users manage their daily consumption of short-form video content (Reels, Shorts, TikToks, etc.) by setting quotas and blocking access when limits are reached.

1. Data collected by ReelGuard

ReelGuard does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All information the App generates (quota settings, usage counters, schedules) is stored locally on your device only using Android SharedPreferences and a local Room database. No account or registration is required.

2. Advertising — Google AdMob

ReelGuard displays banner advertisements provided by Google AdMob (Google LLC, 1600 Amphitheatre Parkway, Mountain View, CA 94043, USA).

To serve relevant ads, AdMob may collect and process:

  • Advertising ID (Android Advertising ID / GAID)
  • Device information (model, OS version, language, time zone)
  • IP address (used to infer approximate location)
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)
  • App usage data relevant to ad targeting

This data is processed by Google in accordance with their own privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy.

You can opt out of personalised advertising at any time in your device settings:
Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID (Android 12+)
or Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation (older versions).

3. Consent (GDPR / EU users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, the App will ask for your consent before displaying personalised ads, in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the ePrivacy Directive. You may withdraw or change your consent at any time by uninstalling the App or resetting your device advertising ID.

When consent is not granted, only non-personalised ads are displayed. ReelGuard itself never accesses or shares your consent choices with any third party other than Google AdMob.

4. Accessibility Service

ReelGuard uses Android's Accessibility Service to detect when a short-form video section is open in a supported app and to count viewed Reels. This service operates entirely on-device. No screen content, keystrokes, or app activity data is recorded, transmitted, or shared with any server or third party.

5. Children's privacy

ReelGuard is not directed at children under 13. The App does not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data through the App, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

6. Data retention

All data stored by ReelGuard (usage statistics, settings) resides on your device. Uninstalling the App permanently removes all associated local data.

7. Your rights

Under the GDPR (for EEA residents) and other applicable privacy laws, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Since ReelGuard does not hold any personal data on external servers, these rights apply primarily to the data held by Google AdMob. To exercise your rights regarding Google's data processing, please visit: https://myaccount.google.com/data-and-privacy.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be published on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
